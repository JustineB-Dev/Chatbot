RECITALS 
WHEREAS, the Educational Institution is engaged in providing quality education to its students and 
preparing them for professional careers; 
WHEREAS, the Company agrees to provide opportunities for students to undertake On-the-Job Training 
(OJT) in various departments within the Company, to gain practical experience related to their academic 
programs; 
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the foregoing premises, the parties hereby agree as follows: 
1. OBJECTIVE 
The objective of this MOA is to establish the terms and conditions under which the Company will provide 
OJT opportunities to students of the Educational Institution. 
2. OBLIGATIONS OF THE EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION 
a. The Educational Institution shall identify and select students who are qualified for the OJT program 
based on the criteria established by the Company and the institution. 
b. The Educational Institution shall provide the students with necessary preparatory training before the 
OJT begins, including guidance on professional conduct, safety procedures, and any other required skills. 
c. The Educational Institution shall assign a faculty or coordinator to oversee the students during the OJT 
period, ensuring that the students' academic progress is not disrupted. 
d. The Educational Institution shall ensure that students comply with the policies and rules of the 
Company during the OJT. 
e. The Educational Institution shall provide the students with necessary documentation such as OJT 
forms, evaluation sheets, and any other paperwork required for completion of the training. 
3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE COMPANY 
a. The Company shall provide students with relevant and meaningful OJT assignments that align with 
their academic programs. 
b. The Company shall assign experienced mentors or supervisors to guide and monitor the students 
throughout the OJT period. 
c. The Company shall provide a safe and conducive working environment, in compliance with health and 
safety standards. 
d. The Company shall ensure that students are not used as substitutes for regular employees and will not 
engage in any work that violates labor laws. 
e. The Company shall provide students with performance evaluations and feedback based on their OJT 
performance, which will be shared with the Educational Institution. 
f. The Company shall not be obligated to pay students for their OJT; however, students may receive 
allowances or stipends at the Company's discretion. 
4. DURATION OF OJT 
The OJT shall commence on [Start Date] and end on [End Date], unless otherwise mutually agreed upon 
by both parties in writing. 
5. CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality regarding any sensitive information they may come across 
during the OJT period. This includes but is not limited to trade secrets, proprietary information, and 
internal business processes. 
6. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY 
a. The Educational Institution shall ensure that the students are covered by adequate insurance for 
accidents or injuries occurring during the OJT period. 
b. The Company shall not be held liable for any injuries or accidents occurring during the OJT, except 
when caused by the negligence of the Company or its employees. 
7. TERMINATION OF AGREEMENT 
Either party may terminate this agreement by providing written notice at least [Number] days in 
advance if the terms of this MOA are not being met or if the OJT students are found to be unsuitable for 
the program. 
8. AMENDMENT 
Any amendments or modifications to this agreement must be made in writing and signed by authorized 
representatives of both parties. 
9. GOVERNING LAW 
This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of 
[Jurisdiction/Country].
